Welcome

Thank you for visiting our website. Our primary affiliation is with the Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology at the University of Washington. We are basic and translational scientists with a shared interest in broadening our knowledge of immunity in the human reproductive tract, how it shapes the course of pregnancy and is in turn shaped by sex hormones, and how it intersects with sexually transmitted pathogens, including HIV. We look back on many years of successful scientific collaboration and intend to continue this path. You will find more information about our joint and individual projects in the Research Section.
