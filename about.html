Vision

We aim to generate new knowledge that will ultimately translate to better prevention and treatment of immunity-driven pregnancy disorders (e.g., preeclampsia, premature birth), sexually transmitted infections (e.g., HIV, herpes simplex, chlamydia), and diseases that are influenced by hormones and biological sex (e.g., autoimmune diseases, inflammatory fluctuations during the menstrual cycle).

Mission

Our team members come from many backgrounds, nationalities, ethnicities, and cultures. Together, we learn and innovate... Maybe someday you will join us?

Motto

As scientists, we dedicate ourselves to uncovering new truths about the natural world... refuse to allow anyone, including ourselves, to become cynical about this motto.
